Our Mission

New York Social Club is a social and charitable club operated under the non-profit organization laws of the state of New York. The mission of the New York Social Club is to provide a safe and welcoming environment for people of all ages and abilities to participate in sports and arts activities. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the joy of physical activity and creative expression, and we are committed to providing quality programs that meet the needs of our members.

Who We Are

A Social & Charitable Club for the Tristate Community

New York Social Club is a social and charitable club operated under the non-profit organization laws of the State of New York. We bring together communities across New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut through the power of sports and socio-cultural activities.
